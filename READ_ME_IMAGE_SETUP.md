# How to set up your College Logo

I've updated the login page to be perfectly centered and ready for your image. Follow these steps to see your logo:

1. **Find your image**: Locate the image you want to use (ensure it's named `college_logo.png`).
2. **Copy the file**: Place this file into the following folder in your project:
   `frontend/public/`
3. **Refresh the page**: The login page will now automatically display your logo in the center of the card.

### Current Settings:
- **Card Alignment:** Perfect center (h-screen + grid place-items-center).
- **Logo Container:** Circular, 160x160px with a soft inset shadow.
- **Theme:** Professional SEC Blue (#002B5B) and Golden Yellow.
